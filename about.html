About the Club

A non-profit men's golf organization serving the Superior community for over 70 years.

Our Mission

The Nemadji Men's Golf Club is a non-profit corporation established by and for its members. Founded in 1952, the club has been the home of competitive men's golf in Superior, Wisconsin for over seven decades. With 390 members, we are proud to be one of the largest and most successful men's golf clubs in the state of Wisconsin.

We exist to organize men's golf leagues and tournaments, maintain a USGA-sanctioned handicap system, promote golf in the community, and enhance the golf experience at Nemadji Public Golf Course. The club is affiliated with the Wisconsin State Golf Association (WSGA). Membership is open to any man 18 years of age or older.

What We Do
⛳ Competitive Leagues

Four men's leagues run throughout the summer season — Bushwhackers (Mon/Tue), Scratch (Tue), Best Ball (Wed), and 40 & Over (Thu). Each league is run by its own commissioner with independent schedules, standings, and playoffs.

🏆 Club Tournaments

The club organizes several tournaments throughout the season, including the Men's Club Spring Scramble, Memorial Weekend Tourney, Tito's Classic, the Men's Club Championship, and the Fall Scramble.

📊 USGA Handicaps

The club maintains a USGA-sanctioned handicap system through GHIN, allowing members to track their index and compete on a level playing field across all leagues and tournaments.

🤝 Community

With 390 members, the Nemadji Men's Golf Club is one of the largest men's golf organizations in the region. We are committed to promoting golf and good sportsmanship in the Superior community.

Membership
How to Join

Membership is open to any man 18 years of age or older. Sign up at the Annual Meeting each spring or contact any club officer for information.

The Annual Meeting and League Sign-Up is held each April — watch the homepage for the current year's date.

Dues & Fees
$60 annual membership

Your $60 annual membership includes:

  • ✓ GHIN handicap membership — track your index all season
  • ✓ Entry into select club tournaments including the Spring Scramble and Fall Scramble
  • ✓ Eligibility to join any of the four Men's Club leagues
  • ✓ Access to all club events and food events throughout the season

Additional league fees apply depending on which league you join. Contact the league commissioner for details. Membership can be purchased at the Annual Spring Meeting each April or at the Pro Shop prior to any league or event.
