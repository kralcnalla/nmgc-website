What is Course Handicap?

Your Course Handicap is a decimal number (e.g., 8.4) that represents the number of strokes you receive on a given course and set of tees.

It's calculated from your Handicap Index adjusted for the Slope Rating and Course Rating of the tees you're playing. Your club or the scorecard will list the Course Handicap for each set of tees.

Enter your Course Handicap exactly as shown — decimals included. The calculator rounds the difference between players to determine how many strokes each player receives per hole.
